Shift Registers + Arduino = MAGIC!

If you are into electronics/arduinos you know how annoying it is to live with just 13 digital outputs. In this article I will introduce you to shift registers, and how you can integrate them into your projects and get (pratically)unlimited digital inputs/outputs by just using 3 of your arduino pins.
